Double Chocolate Chip Cookies


Origin

Michigan


Source

Family recipe


Ingredients

  • Unsalted Butter
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Packed light or dark brown sugar
  • Large egg
  • Pure vanilla extract
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chunks (melted)
  • All-purpose flour
  • Natural unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chunks

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar.
  2. Add the egg and vanilla extract and beat well.
  3. Add the melted chocolate.
  4. In a separate bowl combine the flour, baking soda, cocoa powder and salt.
  5. Combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  6. Add the unmelted chocolate chunks.
  7. Form 15 cookies and place on a baking sheet.
  8. Cooke for 12 to 13 minutes at 350 degrees.

The star of the show in the recipe are the chocolate chips.

Swedish Pancakes


Origin

Sweden


Source

Kristi, True North Kitchen


Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until completely smooth.Transfer to the refrigerator to rest for at least 2 hours or up to 2 days.
  2. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees if you plan to eat the pannkakor right away. Heat a 10 inch nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Stir the batter briefly to recombine.
  4. Brush skillet with melted butter.
  5. Pick the skillet up off of the burner and quickly add Â¼ cup of batter. Immediately begin swirling the batter around the skillet so that it coats the bottom of the pan.
  6. Continue swirling until the batter is just set. Return skillet to the burner.
  7. Cook until the pancake is golden brown in spots on the bottom, about 30 seconds.
  8. Use a thin spatula to loosen the pancake, flip it over continue to cook on the other side for about 30 seconds.
  9. Transfer to a plate. Cover with foil and transfer to oven to keep warm and repeat with remaining batter, wiping out skillet in between pancakes with paper towel as needed.
  10. Serve with lingonberry jam and butter (see note below) or other desired toppings and/or fillings.


Make sure to keep the layer of batter thin to get the correct texture and ease of flipping!

Zucchini Fritters


Origin

Unknown


Source

Nifty Recipe


Ingredients

  • Zucchini
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Vegetable/seed oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper for taste

Steps

  1. Combine eggs, salt, and pepper and whisk.
  2. Add pressed garlic.
  3. Grate the zucchini and squeeze the moisture out of it.
  4. Add zucchini and flour and mix.
  5. Heat oil in pan and add dollops of batter
  6. Serve with or without dipping sauce


This is a quick and easy dish to make, taking less than half an hour!
